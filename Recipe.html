Ingredient

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 7g active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 300ml warm water

    • How to bake

    Instruction
    1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl and let it sit until foamy.
    2. In another bowl, combine flour and salt.
    3. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the flour and stir to form a dough.
    4. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth and elastic
    5. Place the dough in a bowl, cover it, and let it rise until doubled in size.
    6. Punch down the dough, shape it into a loaf, and let it rise again.
    7. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400ºF), then bake the loaf for 25-30 minutes.
    8. Let the bread cool before slicing and serving.
